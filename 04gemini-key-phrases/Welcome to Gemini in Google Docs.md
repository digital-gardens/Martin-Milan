# **Welcome to Gemini in Google Docs**

Welcome to your first experience with Gemini. This integrated AI assistant is designed to help you jumpstart your creative process and streamline information gathering directly within your document.

# **How to Access Gemini**

When you open a new Google Doc, you will likely see a blue pencil icon or a "Help me write" button on the left margin. Clicking this opens a prompt box where you can give instructions to the AI. Note that some advanced features like Smart Compose may be restricted based on your account type.

# **Core Capabilities**

Gemini can handle a variety of requests, ranging from simple drafting to complex research tasks.

## **1\. Generating Content**

You can ask Gemini to create drafts from scratch. For example:

* "Write a paragraph about the benefits of transhumanism."  
* "Draft an email to Person regarding the project timeline."  
* "Create a list of potential gift ideas for Date."

## **2\. Refining and Editing**

If you have existing text, you can highlight it and ask Gemini to:

* **Summarize**: Condense a long section into a few bullet points.  
* **Tone Change**: Make a paragraph sound more professional or more casual.  
* **Bulletize**: Turn a complex paragraph into an easy-to-read list.

## **3\. Integrated Research**

One of the most powerful features is the side panel. By clicking the Gemini icon in the top right corner of the document, you can:

* Ask questions about the current document or other files in your Google Drive.  
* Perform a "web search" style query without leaving the page.  
* Synthesize information from multiple sources, such as comparing details from and regarding your digital emergency plan.

# **Tips for Better Prompts**

To get the most out of Gemini, follow these basic principles:

| Prompt Type | Description | Example |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Direct Instruction** | Clear, verb-driven requests. | "Write a 200-word introduction about..." |
| **Contextual** | Provide background information. | "Based on my notes about File, summarize..." |
| **Persona-based** | Ask Gemini to write from a specific viewpoint. | "As an expert in digital security, explain..." |

# **Privacy and Control**

It is important to manage your data and privacy settings. You can control whether Gemini activity is saved or automatically deleted in your account settings.

If you are using Gemini on a mobile device, please be aware that certain features, such as voice-to-text integration within Docs, may behave differently than on a desktop browser. For extensive brainstorming, you might find Google Keep more flexible for mobile use.
