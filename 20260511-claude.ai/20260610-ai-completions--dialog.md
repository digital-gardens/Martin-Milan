## AI assistant

yes, it is possible to have a dialog with an AI assistant by using the completions as a way to continue the conversation. You can write a prompt, ask the AI to complete it, and then use the completion as a basis for your next prompt. This can create a back-and-forth dialog where you and the AI assistant are building on each other's responses. Just make sure to keep the prompts clear and relevant to maintain a coherent conversation.
